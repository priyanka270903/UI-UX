Rubamin Journey

Rubamin Journey

Top Indian manufacturers and
suppliers of Molybdenum and Zinc


Headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat (India) Rubamin Limited is a USD 250m group with interests in chemicals, metal intermediates, exploration and engineering services, industrial gases production and mineral exploration.

The professional workforce is over 800 strong and consists of top scientists, engineers, accountants and management experts. With more than three decades of operational experience and competence in hydro and pyro-metallurgy processes, Rubamin ensures superior quality products through sustainable chemical processes and the recycling of metal bearing secondary materials.

Rubamin is rated A+ by Indian Credit Rating Agency - CARE and certified Advanced Economic Operator (AEO – Tier I). Rubamin is also an ISO 9001 : 2015 and ISO 14001 : 2015 certified company. The business processes conform to the ISO standard requirements and we ensure that our products are reliable, safe, traceable and of world class quality. At Rubamin, the certification processes are used as strategic tools that reduce costs by minimizing waste and increasing productivity.

Rubamin is a chemicals and metals company that has established itself as a compliant and responsible recycler all over the world, transforming hazardous industrial
waste into useful products.

Rubamin Values

Care

Recycling metals requires state-of-the-art technology, but the health and safety of our employees, customers and community remain the top priority at all times. Risk assessment, training and education are always ongoing processes at Rubamin.

Customize

Quality awareness, training, customer feedback and preventive action help us improve products and processes. Rubamin’s commitment to quality and service has helped maintain our position as a market leader and differentiate ourselves from the competition.

Conserve

Rubamin’s policies and processes reduce the hazardous waste load on landfills and the environmental impact of manufacturing metal compounds from primary sources. Our eco-friendly recycling services and sustainability mission is in compliance with all government regulations.

Collaborate

At Rubamin, we believe in collaborating with our stakeholders and the community. Recycling efforts are aided by our management, employees, suppliers, consultants, research centers and internationally recognized testing agencies.

The Joy of Creating an Institution

Rubamin has been driven by its core purpose – “The joy of creating an institution” - from the start and has evolved into an organisation that all its stakeholders can take pride in. We have loyal and quality oriented customers across all continents who are served through a network of global offices, distributors and warehousing arrangements.

All of our Zinc, Molybdenum and Cobalt products are a result of strong in-house research and development infrastructure supported by state-of-the-art technology. We owe our success to our team of dedicated and motivated employees but also acknowledge that we can be better and that this journey is unending.

People

RWe honour talent, recognize achievements, value contribution and believe that people need to enjoy their work to give their best.

Infrastructure

Each of our units have an independent and self-sufficient lab with expert chemists. Our products are a result of strong in-house research and development infrastructure supported by state-of-the-art technology.

Sustainability

Through continuous R&D we are constantly evolving our techniques to gather valuable metals from waste. The Cradle to Cradle philosophy is at the heart of our processes and methods and that has allowed us to rise to the position of being one of the most sustainable recycling companies in India.
