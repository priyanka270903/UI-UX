We at Rubamin, are dedicated towards maintaining the highest health, safety & environmental standards at all our manufacturing
facilities. We aim to produce materials that minimize negative impact on the environment, utilise industrial waste and other secondary

How Sustainability is Embedded at the Core
of Every Activity at Rubamin

Our think-tanks have given birth to numerous innovative ideas in order to reduce hazardous chemical waste load on landfills, conserve valuable resources and reduce environmental impact of manufacturing metal compounds.Let’s take a tour of some of the most innovative practices at Rubamin.

Recycling of Hazardous Industrial Waste

We are the leading industrial waste management company in Gujarat, and carry out hazardous waste recycling, so as to decrease the load on landfills. While recycling spent catalyst, we are able to recover moly, cobalt, tungsten etc and save valuable natural resources.

Recycling of Used Catalysts

We are one of the leading catalyst recycling companies at the global level, recycling used catalyst to recover embedded metals such as Molybdenum, Cobalt, Nickel, Vanadium, etc. which are rarely found in India, thus contributing to the nation’s total resource efficiency strategy. With this process, we have established ourselves as forerunners in zero waste recycling technology.

Waste Heat Recovery

We capture heat emulating from recycling operations and convert it into steam for downstream applications. This increases competitiveness and minimizes impact on the environment.

Zero Water Waste

We maintain a “zero waste water” discharge system from our recycling process. The sewage wastewater is also used for garden maintenance.

Zero to Landfill

We maintain a “zero to landfill” status to ensure that every element is extracted and we upgrade the process residue to commercial/farm use.

Preserving Air Quality

We continuously monitor emissions and air quality with advanced instruments and display real time readings on electronic boards installed at our factory gates.

Residue Recovery

Our networked facilities ensure that process residue from one facility can be consumed by another for better recovery and waste minimization.

Maintaining Highest Standards of Safety and Health

Our people are our true assets, and we ensure they get the care they deserve. We are persistently working towards eradicating the threats at different levels of operations, by periodically engaging in activities such as HAZOP studies, having strict systems and procedures for work permits, periodic fire drills, safety workshops, etc.
