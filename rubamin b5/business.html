Quality

Our strict adherence to quality is undying and includes a Central Quality Assurance cell for independent monitoring of process/product quality and advanced equipment including ICPs, Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers, Auto Titrators, etc. This supports precise analysis, high traceability through SAP ERP system and relevant quality management certifications.

Infrastructure

Our Pyro & Hydro facilities are located in developed chemical zones that provide government approved effluent treatment and waste disposal infrastructure, skill availability, supporting chemical infrastructure and high level compliance with EHS norms. The versatile and networked production facilities further provide efficient and multi-stage processing of complex raw materials, flexibility in sourcing materials, related cost advantage and the possibility of value-added products with versatile competence. With all such great infrastructure at Rubamin, we have now become reputed and trust zinc oxide producers in India.

Process

The hydro & pyro processing units have experience in Furnace Operations, Statistical Process Control and GMP as well as the capability of Leaching, Purification, Precipitation, Drying and Calcination. Further value addition is created through micronizing, palletizing, morphological controls, surface treatment, etc.

Applications

Rubamin is quickly gaining presence in value-added segments of pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, veterinary industry and sunscreen products with application-specific product variants and quality certifications. We use 99.995% Special High Grade (SHG) Zinc to make high-quality Zinc Oxide and Zinc Carbonate for these sensitive applications.
